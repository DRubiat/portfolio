AtkinsRéalis

As a Graduate Software Engineer at AtkinsRéalis, I've had the opportunity to work on a variety of innovative projects that showcase my technical skills and dedication to improving infrastructure management.

Barton High Level Bridge Application: As part of a team, I contributed to the development of a web application for the Barton High Level Bridge. This application digitises and monitors bridge movements, providing real-time data and insights to ensure the safety and stability of the structure.

Data Scraper Application: I created the Data Scraper application, which extracts data from PDFs and converts it into Excel format. This tool streamlines data management and enhances productivity by automating the extraction process.

Digital Products Catalogue: I developed a Digital Products Catalogue, an online platform that functions like an Amazon for highway and bridge civil engineers. This catalogue allows the highway team to efficiently order and manage assets, improving inventory management and project workflow.
