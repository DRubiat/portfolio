Cardiff University

Personal Website Project: One of my first major projects was designing and developing my own personal website. This project involved creating a user-friendly and visually appealing platform to showcase my portfolio, skills, and achievements. I focused on crafting a responsive design that worked seamlessly across different devices, ensuring an optimal user experience. This project allowed me to experiment with various web technologies and refine my expertise in front-end development.

Pair Project Website: Following the success of my personal website, I collaborated with a fellow student on a pair project. Together, we developed a website for a fictional client, emphasizing teamwork and effective communication. This project challenged us to merge our ideas and skills to create a cohesive, functional, and visually appealing website. We applied principles of user-centered design, ensuring the website met the client's needs and provided an intuitive experience for users.
