Focus Shift Films

Viewfinder for Movies is a mobile application developed for Focus Shift Films, a Cardiff-based film company dedicated to creating content that spreads awareness and showcases creative storytelling. The app guides users to famous movie spots in Wales, such as locations from Doctor Who, Torchwood, and His Dark Materials.

In addition to leading users to these iconic locations, the app includes interactive games that allow users to earn credits. These credits can be spent around Wales, adding an engaging and fun element to the tourism experience.
