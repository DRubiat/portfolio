Weather App

Weather App is a project I developed that provides users with real-time weather information. The app allows users to search for the current weather, temperature, and wind speed for any city. In addition to delivering accurate weather data, the app enhances the user experience by generating a visual background image for the city that the user searches.

The combination of detailed weather information and visually appealing backgrounds makes the app both informative and engaging. Whether users are planning their day or simply curious about the weather in a different location, the Weather App offers a comprehensive and immersive experience.
